// about me

Security-first thinking,
from the stack up

I specialize in PCI DSS compliance and cybersecurity governance — helping organizations understand their risk posture, close compliance gaps, and build programs that actually hold up under scrutiny.

Outside the compliance world, I spend time building out self-hosted infrastructure, writing automation with PowerShell and Bash, containerizing workloads with Docker, and exploring what modern security looks like in a homelab environment.

I believe good security isn't just policy — it's culture, tooling, and the unglamorous work of keeping systems honest over time.
